About

Protein Source Visualizer helps you compare protein sources by normalizing everything to 10g of protein. Each chart lets you explore the trade-offs between calories, cost, and weight (grams) required to hit that protein target.

  • 3D Chart: Calories vs Cost vs Weight per 10g protein.
  • 2D Charts: Pairwise comparisons (Calories–Cost, Calories–Weight, Cost–Weight).
  • Food Sources: Sortable table with the underlying values.

Hover points to see details, click legend items to filter categories, double-click a legend item to isolate it, and use the Reset button on each page to restore the default view.

If you’d like to contribute data corrections or request features, feel free to open an issue or contact the maintainer.
